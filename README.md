# NFT-Marketplace-openDC16
Building my own NFT market Place namely "OpenDC16" where people can buy and sell NFTs with specific DC16 Token on the Internet Computer Blockchain.

The OpenDC16 is NFT Marketplace where NFTs are purchased and minted with Custom made Crypto Token called DC16 - (Decentralised Crypto 16). 
The Decentralised Application is made with React.js Frontend for best user Experience and Motoko backend on the Internet Computer Blockchain Network.

You can Discover new NFTs that are listed for selling on the "discover" route.
You can Mint your own NFT with your own DC16 Tokens on the "Minter" route.
You can check your NFTs list i.e Purchase/Sell on the "My NFTs" route.
